## 1. What Convoyar is (and isn't)

**Convoyar IS:**

- A **coordination** tool for shared rides.
- A place to **organize** trips in private groups (organizations, clubs, schools,
  events) and to **post or request a seat** in public trips.
- Software that suggests who drives whom and at which meeting point.

**Convoyar is NOT:**

- A transportation, taxi, or ride-hailing company.
- The carrier or the trip organizer: **the trip is made by people**, not Convoyar.
- An insurer or a verifier of drivers, vehicles, or passengers.

Convoyar connects people who want to share a ride. Whatever happens during that
ride is the responsibility of the participants.

## 5. How ride coordination works

- You create or join a **trip/event** with an origin, a destination, and a time
  (the trip time is the time you want to arrive at the destination).
- Convoyar suggests **assignments** (who drives whom) respecting limits such as
  vehicle capacity, detour, time window, maximum walk, and declared needs.
- To protect your privacy, **computed meeting points** are shared, not your exact
  home address.
- Suggestions are **guidance**. The final decision to travel, with whom, and under
  what conditions is **always yours and the other people's**.

## 6. Contributions between users (not for profit)

Carpooling is about **sharing costs**, not making money. Convoyar **does not
process payments** between users: any financial arrangement happens directly
between people. In many countries, charging above the shared cost can turn the
trip into commercial transport, with obligations (licenses, insurance, taxes)
that fall solely on the driver. We do not promote or enable for-profit transport.
